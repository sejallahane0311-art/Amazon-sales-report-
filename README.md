# Amazon-sales-report-
# 🛒 Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard: Business Performance Insights
An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Amazon sales data by tracking revenue, orders, profit, customer segments, product categories, and regional performance. The dashboard provides actionable insights through dynamic visualizations, enabling businesses to monitor KPIs, identify sales trends, and make informed strategic decisions.

#  Short Description / Purpose
The **Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard** is a comprehensive Power BI report designed to provide a clear overview of sales performance across products, regions, customer segments, and time periods. It enables business users, sales managers, and analysts to explore key metrics, evaluate profitability, and uncover growth opportunities through interactive dashboards.
#  Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Primary platform used to develop interactive reports and dashboards.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Performed data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Created calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic business metrics.
* 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Built relationships among Sales, Customers, Products, and Date tables for efficient analysis.
* 📈 **Power BI Visualizations** – Used KPI Cards, Line Charts, Bar Charts, Donut Charts, Maps, Tables, and Slicers.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard preview.

# Data Source
**Source:** Amazon Sales Dataset (Sample Business Dataset)
The dataset contains transaction-level sales information, including order details, customer segments, product categories, sales amount, profit, quantity sold, shipping mode, order dates, and regional information. The data is modeled to support multi-dimensional business analysis and interactive reporting.
# ⭐ Features / Highlights
## • Business Problem
E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of sales data every day, making it difficult to monitor business performance and identify trends using spreadsheets alone.
Key business questions include:
* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which regions contribute the most sales and profit?
* How do sales change over time?
* Which customer segments drive the highest revenue?
* What products and markets require business attention?
## • Goal of the Dashboard
The dashboard is designed to:
* Monitor overall sales performance through interactive KPIs.
* Analyze revenue, profit, and order trends over time.
* Compare performance across product categories, customer segments, and regions.
* Enable users to filter and explore data interactively.
* Support data-driven decision-making for sales and business strategy.
## • Walkthrough of Key Visuals
### 📈 Key KPI Cards
Displays important business metrics such as:
* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Order Value
* Profit Margin
### 📅 Sales Trend Analysis
A line chart showing monthly and yearly sales performance to identify growth patterns, seasonal demand, and business trends.
### 📦 Sales by Category
Bar and donut charts compare revenue and profit across product categories and sub-categories, helping identify top-performing products.
### 🌍 Regional Performance
Map and bar charts visualize sales and profit across different regions, states, or countries, highlighting high-performing markets.
### 👥 Customer Segment Analysis
Analyzes sales contribution from Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office customers to understand purchasing behavior.
### 🚚 Shipping Mode Analysis
Shows order distribution by shipping method, helping evaluate delivery performance and logistics.
### 🎛 Interactive Filters
Users can dynamically filter the report by:
* Region
* Product Category
* Customer Segment
* Order Date
* Ship Mode
## •Business Impact & Insights
* Identify top-performing products and profitable categories.
* Evaluate regional sales performance for better market planning.
* Monitor business growth using real-time KPIs.
* Improve inventory and demand forecasting.
* Support strategic pricing and marketing decisions.
* Enhance executive reporting through interactive dashboards.
* Enable faster, data-driven decision-making across sales operations.
